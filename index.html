About Me

Emma has been making videos on her YouTube channel ‘Emma Angeline’ (formerly Sarcastic Fish) since 2013. While the channel initially consisted of comic sketches it has evolved with her, currently featuring primarily lifestyle content: vlogs, travel, books, films and studying.

Emma graduated from King’s College London in 2019 with a Bachelor of Arts in Comparative Literature. She is currently perusing a Master’s degree in Film.
